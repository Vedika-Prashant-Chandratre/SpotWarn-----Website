Frequently Asked Questions

Home Faqs

1.What is meant by Black Spot Region?

"Black spot" generally refers to a specific location or area that stands out due to a higher occurrence of certain events or conditions compared to its surroundings, whether it's related to traffic accidents, crime, signal coverage issues, or other factors.

2.Why a region is declared as Black Spot?

Declaring a region as a black spot serves as a recognition of specific challenges or issues that require targeted interventions and resources to address. It helps prioritize efforts towards improving safety, security, infrastructure, environment, or socio-economic conditions for the benefit of the affected communities.

3.What is Spot Warn App for?

The purpose of the app "Spot Warn" is for identifying and managing black spot areas.

4.How does the App Identify Black Spots?

The app identifies and categorize areas as black spots,on basis user-reported incidents, historical data analysis, or official reports.

5.What Information is Provided About Each Black Spot?

Information on the data or details available for each identified black spot,on basis of zones,cities and reasons.
